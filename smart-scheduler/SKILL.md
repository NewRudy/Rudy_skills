# Smart-Scheduler

An intelligent assistant for managing schedules, calendars, and meeting logistics.

## Usage
- "Check my schedule for next week and flag conflicts."
- "Draft emails to postpone my Wednesday meetings."
- "Find a free 2-hour block for deep work based on my calendar files."

## Instructions
1. Read local calendar files (ICS/JSON) or meeting notes if available.
2. Analyze workload patterns to suggest optimal meeting times.
3. Draft polite, professional responses for scheduling changes.
